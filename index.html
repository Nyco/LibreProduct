Approach

Open source is different

Open source product management is not like startup product management. Different values, different communities, different economics.

01

Community-first

Every product decision starts with the community: contributors, users, and downstream projects. The ecosystem is your product.

02

Transparent by default

Open roadmaps, public backlogs, honest communication. Openness is a feature, not a constraint.

03

Sustainable economics

Build business models that fund development without exploiting community trust. Support, services, open core — done right.
